About

We are a non-profit organization that rescues cats in need. We believe that every animal deserves a dignified life and a loving home. Our shelter was founded on April 12, 2010.

We are a team or responsible people who really loves animals and would do anything to make sure they are safe.

Our cats are living in a clean, big house with garden with big voliere, so the cats can go outside without vandering and risking an accident.

We are supported by our city, and free will sponsorships and gifts.
